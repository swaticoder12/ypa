About Us

Youth Power Academy is an online platform for thousands of aspirants who cannot join a regular coaching institution far from their home due to financial constraints or many other personal problems and for those students also who spent their money in different coaching but could not benefit much.

YPA provides a comprehensive intensive and effective E-learning modules for civil services examination. YPA team will be guiding you regularly.

YPA team will cover the entire syllabus, will provide you the strategy and update you according to the need of the exam. YPA is having experience team which will regularly help you in your journey of success.

Here on YPA platform regular E learning modules are posted of various subjects which are helpful for UPSC and other state civil services exam aspirants especially fresher’s. Modules will be bilingual ( English and Hindi) so that students from both medium can get a benefit from it.

YPA provides necessary study material information/ guidance ( keeping in mind the requirement / syllabus of civil services examination) for better understanding of a subjects. In live sessions of YPA you can directly interact with our team members and clear your doubts.

In future we intend to post more video on diverse topics related to UPSC/State Civil Services exam preparation. For more study material and current affairs related issues please visit our Website and subscribe to our You tube channel so that u don’t miss out any lecture.
